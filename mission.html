What Does My Game Do?

Daily, students and adults get put through immense levels of stress. One of the simplest modes of releiving stress is via games. The game that my team and I will have created is a low intensity fishing game that aims to provide a low risk high reward situation. The sense of accomplishment should act as the ego boost that people need Since it's more exploratory it also works well for friends playing together.

What is a Game to Me?

To me, a game is any type of challenge, physical or mental, regardless of difficulty, with a certain objective. Although games can go on indefinitely, this doesn't rule out that all games have a specific point, ranging from being the last one standing in a shooter game, to collaborating to keep a ball in the air. Personally, when it comes to games, I'm both an achiever and an explorer. I love winning, but more than winning the game I like to experience and learn the ins and outs of the world. That's why I used to love Roblox games, because of all the different worlds. Right now one of my favorite games is Worldbox. The interesting thing about it is because it's a simulator game, you can technically choose your goal, however, ultimately the objective is to be overseer of a world that you create.
